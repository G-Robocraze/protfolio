About Smart Exhaust

The kitchen is one of the most important rooms in any house. There are numerous safety considerations that must be taken while working in the kitchen. Surprising explosions can be caused by a number of things, including uncontrolled fires, rapid temperature increases, the presence of leaks in the gas supply, and others. As soon as possible, the explosions must be located and extinguished. The project's main objective is to identify and address any kitchen safety issues. When there is a significant rise in leakages, the sensor senses it and, the exhaust system turns on, the alarm goes on when the gas level surpasses the recommended amount. Even, the information is notified to the users' mobile phones, by the app. When the temperature in the kitchen is high, the exhaust system ventilates it, lowering the ambient temperature. Finally, by automating the kitchen, this technology enables people to make use of the appliances in the kitchen. All of these controls and, information is accessed using the Blynk app, which can be accessed by smartphones. The goal of this solution is to improve security in the kitchen.
