About MiKa

MiKa is Semi-humanoid robot developed for the Kochi Metro Rail Limited to assist the passengers at the metro station. The robot will be guiding and providing instructions to ensure the safe transport of the passengers. It can also handle the usual FAQs of the passengers and assist them. It can give floor navigation to the passengers and also greet them with a hand gesture. The greetings can be customised according to the age and gender of the passengers. The robot is enables with AI-based speech recognition and will reply back to the passengers. It can also inform the passengers about the train timings and fares. The design of the robot is a 4 wheeled drive with LIDAR and a depth camera for visual perception. The body of the robot are 3D printed and the main controller board is Jetson Xavier board, the motors and the sensors will be controlled by an Teensy4.1. The intended audience is the people who travel by means of Kochi Metro. The audience includes people of all age groups which varies from small children to old people. Phase 1 of the product is set to help the officials of the Human Resource Department in guiding the customers in the metro station in various general FAQs. Also, the product deals with a great scope of engaging robots with people in public places. This could open a wide scope of the interaction of the same and its future scope. The success of the project could avoid the fear of people to interact with robots as well as their functioning.
